Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup salted butter
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1 cup of light brown sugar
  • 2 tsp of pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 tsp of sea salt
  • 1 1/2 cup of chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Line a baking pan with parchment paper and set aside
  2. In a separate bowl mix flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder. Set aside
  3. Cream together butter and sugars until combined
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla until fluffy
  5. Mix in the dry ingredients until combined
  6. Roll 2-3 tbs of dough at a time into balls and place them evenly on your prepared cookie sheets
  7. Bake in preheated oven for approximately 8-10 minutes. Take them out when they are just barely turning brown
  8. Let them sit on the baking pan for 2 minutes before moving to a cooling rack

Chocolate Strawberries

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of fresh strawberries
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Wash strawberries, cut into thin slices and set aside
  2. Melt chocolate chips in a microwave safe bowl in 30 second increments
  3. Melt chips until smooth
  4. Dip dry strawberries in the chocolate and enjoy

Blueberry Coffee

This is one of my favorite drinks and you can make it right at home following these simple steps.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of frozen blueberries
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/8 cup of white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 2 shots of espresso
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 2 tbs of Vanilla Creamer

Instructions

  1. Heat up the blueberries,water and sugar on the stove on medium heat
  2. Heat up until everything is well combined and melted down
  3. Let the mixture cool then blend in the blender
  4. Strain blueberry mixture
  5. Brew espresso and set aside
  6. Fill up with ice
  7. Pour milk and cream in glass
  8. Next pour espresso into the glass
  9. Pour 1/4 cup of the blueberries mixture into the coffee
  10. Now mix and your coffee drink is ready to go

Whipped Cream

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • 1/4 cup of white sugar
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. mix all ingredients into a mixing bowl
  2. mix until soft peaks form
  3. The whipped cream is ready to be topped on any dessert or coffee
