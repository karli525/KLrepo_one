🌧️ Confidence & Setbacks

I used to second-guess every step. Progress felt invisible sometimes — especially when the scale didn’t move. I was chasing perfection and missing progress. Now I realize effort matters more than appearance.

💪 Emotional Wins

This wasn’t just about lifting. It was about trust — in my body, in my discipline, in my worth. For the first time, I stuck to something. I began to believe I could actually follow through — and that made me proud.

🌱 Lessons Learned

  • Progress isn’t always linear — and that’s okay.
  • Rest is strength, not weakness.
  • Discipline builds real confidence.
